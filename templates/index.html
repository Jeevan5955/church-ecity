Who we are?

STATUS OF OUR CHURCH AS AN AFFILIATE OF CHURCH OF SOUTH INDIA : Central Diocese of Church of South India after examination of our progress have considered us as a ’Worshiping Congregation' and temporarily attached to CSI Church Koramangla to take care of all our needs regarding worship services and other Pastoral needs till all formalities for full membership are completed. In the meanwhile CSI. Shanthi Church has come forward voluntarily to sponsor our Church and to help us financially to build our own Church. All our worships are being conducted in Kannada following C.S.I. Order of Worship

Believes

They are united by the Holy Spirit in the body of Christ, of which He is the Head. The true church is manifest in local churches, whose membership should be composed only of believers. The Lord Jesus mandated two ordinances, baptism and the Lord's Supper, which visibly and tangibly express the gospel.

Our creed

I believe in God, the Father almighty, creator of heaven and earth. I believe in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary. He suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died, and was buried; he descended to hell. The third day he rose again from the dead. He ascended to heaven and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty. From there he will come to judge the living and the dead. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy catholic* church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and the life everlasting. Amen.
